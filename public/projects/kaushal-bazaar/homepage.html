What is Kaushal Bazaar

An integrated marketplace ecosystem for skilled India.

Kaushal Bazaar is more than a portal — it's the commerce layer of the Kaushal Ganga ecosystem. A unified platform where skilled entrepreneurs, MSMEs, training partners, and CSR networks meet buyers across B2B, B2C, and D2C channels.

From a weaver in Varanasi to a repair technician in Pune, Kaushal Bazaar gives every skilled enterprise a digital storefront, a verified identity, and access to India's growing opportunity economy.
